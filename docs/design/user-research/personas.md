## ðŸ“ EXAMPLE PERSONAS

### Sarah - Team Lead

**Demographics:**
- Age: 32
- Role: Engineering Team Lead
- Experience: High (10+ years in tech)
- Location: Remote team, US

**Goals:**
- See team progress at a glance
- Unblock team quickly
- Reduce context switching

**Pain Points:**
- Too many tools (Jira, Slack, Email, Docs)
- Lost information in Slack threads
- No visibility into who's working on what

**Behaviors:**
- Checks status 10+ times/day
- Prefers keyboard shortcuts
- Works across 3 monitors

**Quote:**
> "I spend 30% of my day just checking where things are"

---

### Mike - Developer

**Demographics:**
- Age: 26
- Role: Frontend Developer
- Experience: Medium (3 years)
- Location: Office-based

**Goals:**
- Focus on coding
- Clear task requirements
- Quick feedback on PRs

**Pain Points:**
- Unclear requirements
- Long PR review times
- Interruptions from Slack

**Behaviors:**
- Deep work sessions (2-3 hours)
- Uses VS Code + terminal
- Prefers async communication

**Quote:**
> "Just let me code. Don't make me hunt for requirements."
